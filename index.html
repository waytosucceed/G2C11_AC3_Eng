Syllables


Convention/Grammar

Syllables are the beats in words.
Each syllable has one vowel sound.
We can clap, jump, or dance to count syllables.
Some words have one beat, some have two, and some have even more!

Examples:

  • Cat (one syllable)
  • le-mon (two syllables)
  • But-ter-fly (three syllables)
  • El-e-phant (three syllables)
  • Hop (one syllable)
